# Restaurant and Dining Information

Our on-site restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner daily. Room service is also available 24/7.

## Breakfast

- Breakfast is served every morning in the main restaurant.
- Guests can expect a mix of hot and cold items such as bread, pastries, eggs, cereals, fruits, and simple local dishes.
- Drinks include coffee, tea, juice, and water.
- If breakfast is not included in your room rate, it can be added for an additional €12 per person per day. Guests can request this at reception at any time.


## Lunch and Dinner

The restaurant offers:
- A small but varied menu with soups, salads, main dishes, and desserts.
- A combination of local favourites and international options so that both adventurous and cautious eaters can find something suitable.
- Vegetarian choices and some simple options that may be suitable for guests with common dietary preferences.

If you have food allergies or strong restrictions, please inform the staff so they can advise which dishes are suitable.

## Room Service

Room service is available 24/7. During restaurant opening hours, most menu items can be delivered directly to your room. At night, a simplified menu with small meals, snacks, and drinks is available.
A room service delivery fee of €4 applies to each order.
Guests can order room service by calling the restaurant or reception from the room phone, or by using the dedicated number.

## Drinks and Snacks
The restaurant offers a selection of soft drinks, tea, coffee, and other beverages. Prices start at €2.50 for soft drinks and €3 for coffee or tea. Light snacks and small bites are available throughout the day, with prices typically ranging from €4 to €8 depending on the item.

Some Comfort room types may include a small minibar or fridge. Minibar prices vary by item, but standard items usually range between €2 and €5.

## Reservations and Dress Code

For regular days, reservations are usually not required. On particularly busy evenings or during special events, booking a table in advance might be helpful. The atmosphere is relaxed and casual; there is no strict dress code, but guests are asked to wear normal, clean clothing and shoes in the restaurant area.

If you need information about today’s menu, opening hours, or whether a specific dish is available, you can ask directly at reception.
