# Amenities Information

We offer free Wi-Fi in all rooms. TV, AC, and bath depend on the type of room. A gym, a spa, and an indoor swimming pool are also available for extra pay.

## In-Room Amenities

All rooms come with:
- Free high-speed Wi-Fi
- A comfortable bed with fresh linens
- A work desk and chair
- Wardrobe or hanging space for clothes
- Basic toiletries (soap, shampoo, shower gel)
- Fresh towels and a hairdryer

Depending on the **room type and comfort level**, additional amenities may be included:
- Standard rooms typically include a TV with basic channels, a fan or basic AC depending on the season, and a private bathroom with shower.
- Comfort rooms usually offer a larger TV with more channels, individually controlled air conditioning, upgraded bedding, and a slightly larger bathroom, sometimes can be with a bathtub instead of just a shower.

If a specific feature is important to you (for example, bathtub vs. shower, or a larger work area), guests are encouraged to contact reception so we can suggest the most suitable room type.

## Shared Facilities (Paid Extras)

We have several shared hotel facilities that are available to all guests for an additional fee:

Gym – The hotel gym includes basic cardio equipment such as treadmills, exercise bikes, and cross-trainers, along with a small selection of free weights. It is suitable for guests who want a light workout during their stay. The gym is not included in the room price. A day pass costs €8 per person, and guests who prefer unlimited access for the duration of their stay can purchase a full-stay pass for €20 per person.

Spa Area – The spa includes a small relaxation area and two treatment rooms. Guests can book massages and simple treatments directly at the reception or by phone. Spa access, which includes the relaxation area and sauna (if available), costs €12 per person per visit. Massage prices start at €25 for a 30-minute classic massage and €45 for a 60-minute relaxation massage. Availability depends on the daily schedule and must be booked in advance.

Indoor Swimming Pool – The indoor pool is medium-sized and suitable for light swimming or relaxation. Towels are provided for guests in the pool area. Access to the pool is not included in the room rate. The cost is €10 per person per visit. Guests can also purchase a combined pool and gym pass for €15 per person per day.

All facility charges can be added directly to the room bill and paid at check-out. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult in the pool area. Towels are provided for spa and pool use, while gym use requires bringing your own towel or requesting one at reception.


## Other Practical Amenities

- **Luggage storage** at reception, free on the day of arrival and departure.
- **Iron and ironing board** available on request.
- **Extra pillows or blankets** can be provided upon request, subject to availability.
- **Daily housekeeping** is provided for stays longer than one night. Guests who prefer not to be disturbed can use the “Do Not Disturb” sign.

If you are unsure whether a specific amenity is available in your room type, feel free to ask. The reception team can check your booking details and confirm what is included.
