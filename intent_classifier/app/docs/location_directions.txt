# Location and Directions

We are located in the city center, just a 5-minute walk from the main train station. Taxis and public transport are nearby, and major museums and attractions are within walking distance.

## City Center Location

The hotel is situated in a central area, making it convenient for both business and leisure travellers. Many cafes, restaurants, and shops can be reached in a few minutes on foot. Because of the central position, guests do not necessarily need a car to move around the city.

## From the Main Train Station

- Walking time to the hotel: approximately **5 minutes**.
- The route is straightforward and mostly flat, suitable for rolling luggage.
- Signage from the station area makes it easy to find the hotel.

For guests with heavy luggage or limited mobility, a short taxi ride from the station is also possible and usually inexpensive due to the short distance.

## From the Airport

- Taxis and ride-hailing services can also bring you directly from the airport to the hotel entrance. Travel time depends on traffic but is usually reasonable.

## Public Transport

- The hotel is surrounded by several **bus and tram stops** within a short walking distance.
- These lines connect you to residential districts, business areas, shopping centrers, and other parts of the city.
- Tickets for public transport can often be bought via machines, apps, or at nearby kiosks.

If you are not sure which line to take, the reception team can suggest the easiest route for your plans.

## Nearby Attractions

Because of the central location, a number of key points of interest can be reached on foot:
- Main city square and historic centre.
- Popular museums and galleries.
- Parks and riverside walking areas.
- Shopping streets and local markets.

Reception can provide a small city map or recommend walking routes depending on your interests.

## Arriving by Car

Guests arriving by car can follow the directions in their navigation app by searching for the hotel name or the street address. Once you arrive at the property, you can use the underground parking (for an extra fee) or stop briefly in the drop-off area to check in and unload luggage before parking.
